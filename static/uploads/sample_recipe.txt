Crunchy Hazelnut Muesli Recipe

Ingredients:
- Rolled oats: 40%
- Hazelnuts (chopped): 15% 
- Dried cranberries: 12%
- Pumpkin seeds: 8%
- Sunflower seeds: 7%
- Honey: 10%
- Coconut flakes: 5%
- Cinnamon: 2%
- Sea salt: 1%

Nutritional Information (per 100g):
Energy: 420 kcal
Fat: 18.5g
Carbohydrates: 55.2g
Protein: 11.8g
Fiber: 9.2g
Salt: 0.8g

Allergens:
- Contains nuts (hazelnuts)
- May contain traces of other tree nuts
- May contain gluten

Health Claims:
- High in fiber
- Source of protein
- Contains omega-3 fatty acids
- Natural antioxidants from cranberries
- No artificial preservatives

Production Notes:
This premium muesli blend combines the nutty crunch of hazelnuts with the sweetness of dried cranberries. The oats provide sustained energy while the seeds add essential nutrients. Perfect for breakfast or as a healthy snack.
